    Zlateh the Goat

    Should Aaron’s father keep Zlateh, the goat? Using the story Zlateh the Goat and Other Stories by Isaac Bashevis Singer, students will work in small groups to create an argument with supporting statements, and then present their position in front of the class in a mock trial setting.
